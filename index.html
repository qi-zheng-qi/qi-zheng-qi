Portfolio

High-Precision Geographic Information Recognition

In the field of remote sensing, the random forest algorithm has proven its effectiveness in machine learning classification tasks. My core research work focused on exploring how to combine medium-resolution landsat satellite data with the random forest algorithm to acquire and simulate long time series of land use and land cover changes in complex terrain areas. This research is significant for understanding and predicting land use changes, especially in complex terrain areas like Beijing. I used Landsat 8 satellite multispectral data and applied the random forest classification method to conduct an in-depth study on land use types in the Beijing area. I extracted spectral and texture information from Landsat 8 data to construct an optimal feature set, assessing the applicability of the random forest method in these areas.

Computational Design Based on Audio

Vienna, known as the world's capital of music, seems to have symphonies and melodies filling every street and alley. Inspired by this city, I designed an interactive musical installation based on computational design. It generates dynamic music waveforms and visual patterns through algorithms. The system can capture environmental sounds and interact with people's body movements, creating a flowing "container" of music, environment, and people. By using Fourier transform and time-domain signal analysis, the installation analyzes external audio signals in real-time and translates them into dynamic visual expressions, allowing participants to feel the dialogue between music and space.

Environmental Assessment and Index Correlation Analysis of Tianjin Metro Station Area

This project aimed to address the environmental quality assessment of areas surrounding Tianjin Metro stations. I utilized deep learning techniques to analyze 30,000 street view images for semantic segmentation, which allowed us to understand the spatial distribution of various environmental elements. We then developed a set of statistical methods to score the environmental data, facilitating the quantification of environmental quality. The project resulted in the creation of a rule set to aid in urban environmental improvement, contributing to the sustainable development of urban spaces.
